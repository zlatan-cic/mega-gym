Our Story

Our story began with a single vision: to create a gym where everyone feels welcome and supported. We wanted to create a space where people could come to work out, to learn, and to grow.

Open Dorr Policy

We believe that everyone has the potential to achieve their fitness goals. That's why we have an open door policy. Our trainers are always here to help you, no matter your fitness level or experience.

Fully Insured

At Mega Gym, we are fully insured, so you can rest assured that you are protected in the event of an accident or injury. We are committed to providing our members with a safe and enjoyable workout experience.

Personal Trainer

Our personal training programs are designed to help you reach your fitness goals in the safest and most efficient way possible. Our experienced and certified trainers will work with you one-on-one to create a personalized fitness plan that meets your individual needs and goals.
